About Frontbits

A small studio, built for ambitious work.

We're not a fifty-person agency with three rounds of approvals and a junior team doing the actual work. We're a small, senior, founder-led studio, and that's on purpose.

Why we exist

Most agencies slow you down. We don't.

Frontbits started from a simple frustration: agencies that take months to ship software that should take weeks, charge enterprise rates for junior execution, and disappear the moment the invoice is paid.

We build the opposite. Every project goes through the same small team from kickoff to launch, so nothing gets lost in translation between a salesperson, a project manager, and whoever's actually doing the work. You talk to the person building your product. Every time.

That's not a scale problem we haven't solved yet. It's the model. Small, senior, and accountable, on purpose.

How we work

The principles behind every project.

Speed without shortcuts

We ship in one to two weeks because we cut bureaucracy, not corners. Fewer meetings, faster decisions, same craft.

One team, start to finish

The people who design your project are the people who build it. No handoffs, no re-explaining yourself to someone new.

Outcomes over outputs

We measure success in leads, conversions, and revenue, not pages delivered or hours billed.

You own everything

Your domain, your codebase, your content. No lock-in, no hostage situations if you ever want to leave.
