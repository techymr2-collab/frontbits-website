FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do you only build custom software?

Yes. We focus exclusively on custom software, internal tools, SaaS dashboards, automations, and web apps tailored to your workflow. We no longer take on marketing website or ecommerce projects, so every hour goes into software that runs your business.

How long does a project take?

Most custom software projects ship in 4 to 8+ weeks depending on scope, small internal tools can move faster. You'll get a concrete timeline in your proposal, and we hit it.

How much does it cost?

Every project is quoted as a fixed price before we start, based on scope, so there are no hourly surprises. Book a free call and you'll have an exact proposal within 48 hours. If scope changes mid project, we requote transparently first.

Do you handle onboarding and UX copy too?

Yes. Words matter in software too, so we don't design around lorem ipsum. We shape your onboarding flows and in-app copy with you as part of the project, which you can refine at review stages.

Can I make changes myself after launch?

Absolutely. We hand over full documentation and record a short walkthrough of the codebase, so your team can make small changes with confidence. Prefer not to touch it? Our care plans handle updates for you.

What platforms do you build on?

Whatever genuinely fits: modern stacks (Next.js/React/Node/TypeScript), Postgres or Supabase for the backend, and whatever your team already runs for integrations. We recommend based on your goals, never on what's easiest for us.

What if I don't like the design?

You see the design before a single line of code is written, with revision rounds built into every package. We iterate until it's right, clients approve every milestone before we move forward.

Do you work with clients outside your country?

Yes, most of our clients are remote, across the US, UK, Europe, and Australia. Everything runs async friendly with scheduled calls at times that work for your timezone.
