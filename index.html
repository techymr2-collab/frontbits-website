FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How long does a website take?

Landing pages typically ship in 1 to 2 weeks, business websites in 2 to 4 weeks, and web apps in 4 to 8+ weeks depending on scope. You'll get a concrete timeline in your proposal, and we hit it.

How much does it cost?

Landing pages start at $990 and business websites at $2,490. Every project is quoted as a fixed price before we start, so there are no hourly surprises. If scope changes mid project, we requote transparently first.

Do you write the copy too?

Yes. Words sell, so we don't design around lorem ipsum. We shape your messaging with you and write conversion focused copy as part of the project, which you can refine at review stages.

Can I update the site myself after launch?

Absolutely. Business websites ship with a CMS, and we record a short walkthrough so editing pages, posts, and images is easy. Prefer not to touch it? Our care plans handle updates for you.

What platforms do you build on?

Whatever genuinely fits: modern custom stacks (Next.js/React), Webflow or Framer for marketing sites, and Shopify for ecommerce. We recommend based on your goals and team, never on what's easiest for us.

What if I don't like the design?

You see the design before a single line of code is written, with revision rounds built into every package. We iterate until it's right, clients approve every milestone before we move forward.

Do you work with clients outside your country?

Yes, most of our clients are remote, across the US, UK, Europe, and Australia. Everything runs async friendly with scheduled calls at times that work for your timezone.
